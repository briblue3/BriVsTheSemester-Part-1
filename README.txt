# BriVsTheSemester-Part-1
Plants vs Zombies Remake

How to play the game:
- Arrow keys are preferred player control method.
- Click on a backpack to pick it up, press the space bar to throw it.
- Once a backpack is thrown, a new one is created in the locker space.
- If 5 books cross the left edge of the screen, you lose the game.
- If you lose all 5 hit points (from being hit by books), you lose the game.
- Pass all 15 waves and you win!
